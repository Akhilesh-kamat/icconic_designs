39 Park Dr

Quick Facts

New Single Family House

Style: Modern Farmhouse

Current Stage: Open House
4,800 Sq Ft of living space

6 Bedrooms / 5.5 Bathrooms / 2 Car Garage

Builder's 10 Year Warranty (10-2-1)

At a glance

Welcome to this bright and spectacular home! This six-bedroom modern farmhouse is located in the heart of Livingston. With over 4,800 sq ft of living space on three levels, this move-in ready house offers elegance and all the amenities one could want in luxury living. An inviting front covered porch introduces the entry foyer, which is flanked by a formal dining room accented by custom molding and complete with a large butler's pantry with plenty of cabinets, a sink and a beverage center.

The highlight of the main level’s open floor plan is the gorgeous chef’s kitchen that boasts an oversized quartz island, high end GE Cafe SS appliances, custom cabinets, and spacious walk in pantry. The adjacent family room has high ceilings and a high-end 50" linear fireplace. The family room has access to covered outdoor porch which allows for everyday enjoyment and large scale entertainment. The main level has a bedroom with ensuite bath, powder room with exquisite design accents, closet and a mud room that connects the 2 car garage.

Ascending through the staircase to the second story, this  level offers a spacious primary suite, complete with tray ceilings and large windows. The suite also contains his and hers walk-in closets and a spa inspired primary bathroom with a spacious glass shower, separate soaking tub and double vanity. This level also has three generously sized bedrooms – Two bedrooms share a Jack and Jill bathroom, and the 4th bedroom has its own en-suite bathroom.  The large laundry room conveniently located on this level has extra storage space, built-in cabinetry.

 

The fully finished lower level offers an au-pair friendly bedroom with a large closet and windows. The media / home theater room has plenty of space to host a movie or game night and has a built-in AV center. The lower level also has a full bathroom, dry bar with cabinetry, an enormous recreation / living area and a mechanical room.
